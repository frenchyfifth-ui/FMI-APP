# AI Layer

AI is scoped as a tool for **modeling, synthesis, and stress-testing**.

## Rules of Engagement
1.  **Assistant, Not Authority:** AI does not make normative decisions.
2.  **No Optimization for Optimism:** AI must surface failure modes first.
3.  **Buffer Zone:** AI inputs are treated as "Raw Buffer" material until distilled into constraints.

AI is used to combat the "Buffer Trap" by distilling infinite noise into finite constraints.
