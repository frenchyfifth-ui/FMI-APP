# Problem Statement

Global human systems depend on tightly coupled supply chains, centralized energy production, and abstract financial instruments. These dependencies increase fragility under environmental, geopolitical, and climatic stress.

**The Cognitive Failure Mode:**
Individuals operating in high-complexity environments often suffer from "Cognitive Paralysis" or "Infinite Abstraction Loops," where the generation of models outpaces the execution of artifacts.

**The Objective:**
This project asks whether specific systems—possessing structural characteristics of **Constraint-First Design**—can allow partial decoupling while maintaining ecological, human, and execution viability.
