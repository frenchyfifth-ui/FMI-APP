# Core Hypothesis (Stage 01)

The Mascarene region (and systems modeled upon its constraints) possesses a unique combination of ecological stability, geographic isolation, and manageable scale.

**Hypothesis:**
Such systems can function as a partial refuge for human and non-human systems under global stress conditions, **provided strict constraints on energy, finance, and population dynamics are respected.**

## Falsifiability
This hypothesis is false if:
1.  Ecological productivity collapses under modeled stress.
2.  Minimum energy needs cannot be met without external dependency.
3.  Financial isolation leads to rapid systemic failure.
4.  Execution throughput drops to zero (Deadlock).
