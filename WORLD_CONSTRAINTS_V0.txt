# Minimal, actionable execution spine (v0)

## Modes (One at a time)
THINK | BUILD | MAINTAIN | HUMAN
- Cross-mode activity = error, stop.

## Daily Alignment (15 min)
- 1 completed action
- 1 executable priority
- 1 support need
No discussion; update JOURNEY_LOGS.

## Execution Rule
- New ideas require an external artifact first (file, commit, deliverable).

## Hard Done
- DONE = observable, external, boring
- Anything exciting → not done.

## Minimum Human Baseline
- Sleep
- Eat
- Move
- Speak to another human

## Failure Handling
- If stalled >72 hours → cut scope 50%, execute immediately
- No debate, no exception
